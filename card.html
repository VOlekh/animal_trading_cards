Velociraptor

Velociraptor are well known for their role in the 1990 novel Jurassic Park by Michael Crichton and its 1993 film adaptation, directed by Steven Spielberg.*

  • Size: 1,8 m long, 0,7 m height
  • Weight: up to 20 kg
  • Skull: was up to 25 cm long
  • Bone outgrowths: stretched along from 4-10 vertebrae

Velociraptor (/vɪˈlɒsɪræptər/; meaning "swift seizer" in Latin) is a genus of dromaeosaurid theropod dinosaur that lived approximately 75 to 71 million years ago during the latter part of the Cretaceous Period.*
